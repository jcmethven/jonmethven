About

I live in New York City with my wife and sons. My work has appeared in Timothy McSweeney's Internet Tendency, The New York Times, The Atlantic, New York Magazine, Buzzfeed, New York Observer, Daily Beast, The Morning News, n+1 and The Awl. I am the author of three novels: THERAPY MAMMALS (2018), STRANGE BOAT (2016), AND THIS IS YOUR CAPTAIN SPEAKING (2012).
